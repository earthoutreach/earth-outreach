Here's a near-complete list of the most commonly used styles options for point, lines & polygons. Try playing with the values to change the way your features look.

KML colors are in alpha, blue, green, red sequence, unlike HTML colors which are red, blue, green. Here is a website that will help you pick KML colors: KML color picker

Or you can color a polygon in Google Earth, and copy out the color value from the KML source code.
